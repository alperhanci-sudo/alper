We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our longevity and health-tracking services to you.

User-Provided Data: We may collect personal and health-related information that you voluntarily provide, such as age, gender, and specific medical or laboratory test results used within the App to generate insights or health scores.

We use the collected data for various purposes, including:

3. Apple HealthKit Privacy
We strictly adhere to Apple's Developer Program License Agreement regarding health data.

We do not use or disclose to third parties any data gathered from HealthKit for advertising, marketing, or other use-based data mining purposes.

We do not sell your HealthKit data to any third party.

HealthKit data is processed locally on your device or securely transmitted solely for the purpose of providing the App's health and fitness features.
